Nyano Kakh

Smart Blanket for Infant Safety

About the Project

What is Nyano Kakh?

Nyano Kakh is a smart baby blanket that uses IoT sensors to monitor real-time temperature, heart rate, and SpO₂ of infants. The system automatically activates a heating mechanism if the temperature drops too low.

The Problem

Infant mortality remains a significant challenge in many rural and under-resourced areas. Many of these deaths are preventable with proper monitoring and timely intervention.

Our Solution

By creating an affordable, accessible smart blanket, we aim to provide real-time monitoring and automatic temperature regulation to help reduce infant mortality rates and give parents peace of mind.

Design & Technology

Challenges

  • Creating a comfortable, safe blanket with embedded sensors
  • Ensuring accurate health monitoring in various environments
  • Developing reliable temperature regulation mechanisms
  • Making the technology affordable and accessible
  • Ensuring long battery life and ease of maintenance

Our Solution

We've designed a smart blanket that seamlessly integrates sensors while maintaining comfort and safety. The system includes:

  • Temperature sensors with automatic heating regulation
  • Heart rate and SpO₂ monitoring
  • Real-time data transmission to caregiver devices
  • Alert system for abnormal readings
  • User-friendly interface for monitoring

Impact & Market Fit

Market Analysis

While there are various baby monitors on the market, Nyano Kakh offers a unique combination of features that address critical gaps:

  • Most existing solutions focus on audio/video monitoring rather than vital signs
  • Current medical-grade monitors are expensive and not accessible to many communities
  • Few solutions offer automated temperature regulation
